## What "gap = 0" honestly means (the directive's "의도적 비연결 명시")
- **Every real engine is production-reachable** — directly wired (136), transitively via a wired package (362,
  e.g. all 73 `catalog/*`, 55 `pillar3/*`, 48 `mathmode/*`, 31 `accel/*`, 31 `recall/*`, 28 `extract/*`, 17

- ★ This is **reach**, NOT a fold-rate multiplier (RF-1): the ~6.8% real-world foldable ceiling is structural and
  **unchanged**. "651 engines wired" is NOT "coverage explosion".
